Built for focus.

FocusForge is designed to help you stop procrastinating and actually finish what you start.

The Problem

Most people struggle to stay focused. Distractions are everywhere, and motivation fades quickly. FocusForge removes the noise and forces you into action.

The Method

FocusForge is built around the Pomodoro technique — short, intense focus sessions. You can choose:

  • 15 minutes → quick tasks
  • 25 minutes → standard deep focus
  • 45 minutes → long sessions

Why It Works

Instead of relying on motivation, FocusForge builds discipline. Streaks, XP, and session tracking push you to stay consistent.

The Goal

The goal is simple: show up every day, do the work, and build momentum.
